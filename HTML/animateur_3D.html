2D/3D Animator

HIS TASK

This animator works both on 3D animation and 2D animation. They create an extensive series of images that form the animation seen in movies, commercials, television programs, and video games. The images can be made up of digital or hand-drawn pictures, models or puppets. Animators are generally specialized in one specific area, such as characters, scenery or backgrounds design. They usually work on computer software. The basic skill of animation relies heavily on artistic ability, but there is an increasing need for animators to be familiar with technical computer packages with the evolution of technology. They are artists that work off-scene. They can work as freelancers or work in a company. About half the animators work remotely and from their home. They have a lot of responsibilities that vary depending on their workplace.
